01 Forecasting Engine

Multi-Variable Predictive Forecasting & Risk Engine

Developed to ingest volatile operational metrics — including production_efficiency, resource_utilization, and throughput_velocity — from historical_performance_data.csv, analyze system variance (R² = 0.60), and output optimized capital allocation percentages using automated statistical regression modeling and Kelly Criterion risk management.

  • Multi-variable linear regression with StandardScaler feature normalization
  • Hold-out evaluation reporting MSE, RMSE, and R² on the test partition
  • Forward predictions with 95% confidence intervals and residual variance (σ²)
  • Fractional Kelly allocation capped at 25% for enterprise risk governance

02 Reconciliation Pipeline

Automated Ledger Reconciliation & ETL Pipeline

Designed to solve the administrative overhead of manual transaction matching by automatically ingesting, standardizing, and checking unaligned data streams — external_vendor_dump.csv versus internal_general_ledger.csv — for structural discrepancies. The pipeline performs outer-join reconciliation, flags AMOUNT_MISMATCH variances and LEDGER_ONLY / VENDOR_ONLY omissions, and exports all exceptions to recon_variance_report.csv.

  • Automated currency parsing, date normalization, and whitespace cleaning
  • Flexible column alias resolution across heterogeneous CSV schemas
  • Outer-join matching on transaction ID with date + amount fallback keys
  • Auditable exception report sorted by status for finance team review
